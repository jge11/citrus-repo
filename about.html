Citrus Sky Web Designs

My name is Javier G. I am the sole owner and developer of Citrus Sky Web Designs. I got into programming when I wanted to have a skill that would allow me to build cool things for fun as a hobby at first. Little did I know this would then start my career as a Software Engineer. After working in the industry for 2 years and then being let go I decided that I wanted to take this new found skill and help out smaller businesses that need a website or needed to update their current one but don't want to spend a ton of money they would get charged by a big agency.

What I noticed from talking to small business owners is that they can't put the upfront cost of thousands of dollars on a new website or updating their current one. Or were almost about to get taken advantage of by someone in their mother's basement and hasn't touch grass in the last 5 years. Being in a undesirable position they were left with not being able to afford a website or not willing to pay for one they did not like at all. This is where I saw an opportunity and created a Web Agency with a business model to cater to this demographic. By putting $0 down and paying $200 a month a small business can manage to get a website that is not only responsive on a computer but is also responsive on cell phones without having to sell and arm and a leg.
